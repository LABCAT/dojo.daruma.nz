# Play Store Listing ASO Copy

Use this copy when setting up the store listing for Multiplication Dojo in the Google Play Console.

## Text Assets

**App Title (Max 30 chars)**
Multiplication Dojo: Math Game

**Short Description (Max 80 chars)**
Master times tables in the dojo! A fast-paced, arcade math game for all ages.

**Full Description (Max 4000 chars)**
Master times tables and sharpen your mental math skills in the Multiplication Dojo! Whether you're a student learning multiplication for the first time or an adult looking to keep your mind sharp, the Dojo provides a fun, arcade-style training ground to test your limits.

Step into the dojo and choose your path:
• Ashigaru (Beginner): Start your journey with basic 1–10 times tables.
• Samurai (Intermediate): Challenge yourself with numbers up to 20.
• Ronin (Advanced): Push your limits with difficult double-digit problems up to 50.
• Shogun (Master): The ultimate test of mental math with numbers up to 100.

Earn Your Weapons
Progress through the Dojo by passing 20-question challenges. Answer fast and accurately to unlock 5 legendary weapon ranks for each path: Bokken, Tanto, Wakizashi, Katana, and the ultimate Nodachi. Can you achieve the title of Master?

Practice Mode
Need to warm up before a challenge? Enter Practice Mode for an infinite stream of questions. Build up your streak combo, learn from your mistakes without pressure, and train your muscle memory. 

Why Multiplication Dojo?
• 100% Offline: Play anywhere, anytime without an internet connection.
• No Ads, No Interruptions: A pure, focused learning environment.
• No Account Required: Jump straight into the action immediately.
• Beautiful Arcade Aesthetic: Enjoy responsive UI, satisfying animations, and dynamic colors that make math feel like an exciting game.

Your training begins now. Step into the Multiplication Dojo!
